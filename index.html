About the Firm

Led by Dr. Tina Wilson, CPA

Tina Wilson CPA PC was incorporated in the State of Georgia in 2006 and serves individuals and small businesses throughout the greater Metro Atlanta area. While headquartered in Fayetteville, the firm works with clients across the Atlanta metropolitan region. Dr. Wilson became a Certified Public Accountant in 2004 and brings over 20 years of experience in accounting, tax strategy, and financial advisory services.

As a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the Georgia Society of CPAs, Dr. Wilson upholds the profession’s highest standards of integrity, diligence, and objectivity. The firm is also a certified WBE/MBE.

FAQ

Answers before you ask

If you don’t see your question here, send it in the contact form and we’ll respond quickly.

Do you take new clients year-round?

Yes — subject to capacity. Tax planning and monthly accounting are often easiest to start outside peak season, but we can onboard anytime.

Do you work remotely or in-person?

Both. Most work is remote and secure, and we schedule in-person meetings when it adds value, especially for kickoff or complex planning.

What software do you support?

QuickBooks Online is common, and we can adapt to your stack. If you’re not sure, we’ll recommend a simple setup that matches your needs.

Can you help with tax notices or IRS letters?

Yes. We handle notice response, document gathering, and communication so you’re not navigating it alone.
